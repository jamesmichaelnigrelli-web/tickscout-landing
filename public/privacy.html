Privacy Policy

Effective June 11, 2026 · Last updated June 11, 2026

TickScout HQ ("TickScout," "we," "us") operates the TickScout mobile app and the website tickscouthq.com (together, the "Services"). This policy explains what we collect, why, how it's shared, and the choices you have. Questions or requests: support@tickscouthq.com.

The short version. Most of your data stays on your phone. We collect your account email and the tick photos you choose to scan. Tick photos are sent to our AI provider only to identify the tick — not to identify you. We do not collect your location, we use no advertising or third‑party analytics, and we do not sell your data.

2. How we use your information

To create and secure your account; to identify ticks and show species information; to let you track pets and review past scans; to provide optional lab and telehealth options if you choose them; and to prevent abuse and meet legal obligations.

3. How tick photos are processed

When you scan a tick, the photo is sent over an encrypted connection to our backend (Google Cloud Functions), which forwards it to Anthropic, PBC (the Claude AI service) solely to identify the tick. The photo is processed to generate your result and is handled under Anthropic's commercial data‑processing terms. We do not use your tick photos to train models, and we do not use them to identify you. A signed‑in account is required to scan, which lets us prevent abuse of the service.

5. Data storage & security

  • Most data (profile, pets, scan history) is stored locally on your device in app‑private storage.
  • Account credentials are managed by Firebase Authentication.
  • Secrets used to call third‑party services are held server‑side only and are never embedded in the app.
  • All network calls use encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS); sign‑in attempts are rate‑limited to deter abuse.

No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect your information.

6. Data retention

Local data stays on your device until you delete it in the app, clear the app's data, or uninstall. Account data is retained by Firebase until the account is deleted. Tick photos are retained only as needed to process and return your result. To request deletion of your account or data, email support@tickscouthq.com.

7. Your choices & rights

  • Access / deletion: request a copy or deletion of your account data via support@tickscouthq.com.
  • Local data: delete pets, profile, and history in‑app, or clear the app's data.
  • Permissions: revoke the Camera permission in system settings (scanning won't work without it).
  • California residents: you have rights to know, delete, and correct your personal information, and not to be discriminated against for exercising them. We do not sell or share personal information for cross‑context behavioral advertising. To exercise these rights, contact support@tickscouthq.com.

8. Children's privacy

The app is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their personal information. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact support@tickscouthq.com and we will delete it.

9. Health information disclaimer

TickScout provides informational tick‑identification content only. It is not a medical device, is not diagnostic, and does not provide medical advice. Information you enter is not treated as protected health information under HIPAA, and TickScout is not a HIPAA‑covered entity. Always consult a qualified healthcare provider for medical decisions.

10. International users

The Services are operated from the United States. If you use them from outside the U.S., you consent to processing your information in the United States, which may have different data‑protection laws than your country.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Material changes will be posted here with a new "Last updated" date and, where appropriate, surfaced in the app.
